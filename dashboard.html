Cyber Awareness

Common Scam Types

  • Phishing emails & messages
  • Fake lottery/win scams
  • OTP or banking scams
  • Tech support scams

How Phishing Works

Phishing tricks users into sharing sensitive info (passwords, OTPs, bank details) using fake emails, websites, or messages.

Red Flags Checklist

  • Urgent action requested
  • Unexpected links or attachments
  • Requests for passwords or OTPs
  • Suspicious sender or domain

Safety Tips

  • Verify links before clicking
  • Never share passwords or OTPs
  • Use 2FA whenever possible
  • Report suspicious messages
