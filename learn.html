Cyber Awareness

Common Scam Types

  • Phishing emails and messages
  • Fake lottery/win scams
  • OTP or banking scams
  • Tech support scams

How Phishing Works

Phishing is when attackers trick users into giving sensitive info like passwords, bank details, or OTPs using fake emails, websites, or messages.

Red Flags Checklist

  • Urgent action requested
  • Unexpected links or attachments
  • Requests for passwords, OTPs, or personal info
  • Suspicious sender or domain

Safety Tips

  • Verify links before clicking
  • Never share passwords or OTPs
  • Use 2FA wherever possible
  • Report suspicious messages
